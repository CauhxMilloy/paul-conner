Multi-viewtube was a site that I made mid-2012.
The site took me about 3 days to make. I'm really happy with how the site turned out. This was the first time I really had used Javascript, and I used this project as a means to teach it to myself. I've always prefered compiled languages (like C/C++), but variety is fun.

My main motivation to make this site was to watch all the yogscast minecraft hungergames clips at the same time.
I couldn't find any other sites that let me do that many videos - nor would they let me synch the vids properly. So, I made this site.
