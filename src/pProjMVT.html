Multi-viewtube was a site that I made mid-2012.
The site took me about 3 days to make - at some point, I'd like to go back to it and freshen it up.
I'm really happy with how this site turned out.
This was the first time I really had used Javascript, and I used this project as a means to teach it to myself.
My knowledge of web-based languages has grown a lot since then, and I can't wait to update the site.
My main motivation to make this site was to watch all the yogscast minecraft hungergames clips at the same time.
I couldn't find any other sites that let me do that many videos - nor would they let me synch the vids properly.
So, I made this site.

I hope you enjoy it:
